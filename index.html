What is LIMINA?

LIMINA is a conditional buy order checkout service that merchants and businesses integrate into their platforms. We enable customers and B2B buyers to commit to purchase when specific conditions are met - turning abandoned carts and unmet procurement needs into future guaranteed sales.

For Shoppers

Commit to buy at your ideal price. We automatically complete the purchase when conditions are met - no more missing deals or checking prices daily.

For Merchants

Convert price-sensitive browsers into committed buyers. See real demand at different price points and capture sales you'd otherwise lose.

For B2B

Facilitate conditional B2B transactions. Buyers can submit binding purchase orders with specific price, quantity, and timeline conditions. Sellers can manage these commitments, unlocking new sales channels and improving demand forecasting. Supports MOQ aggregation and group buys.

How Buy Orders Work

A simple, transparent process for B2C and B2B

For Retail Shoppers (B2C):

1

Set Conditions

Shoppers specify their purchase conditions: target price, timeframe, or inventory availability for desired products.

2

Monitor & Match

LIMINA monitors conditions in real-time. When criteria are met (e.g., price drop), we instantly notify and prepare for execution.

3

Automatic Execution

Purchase completes automatically. Customer gets their deal, merchant gets a sale they might have missed.

For B2B Transactions:

1

Buyer Initiates Conditional PO

Businesses submit detailed conditional purchase orders (price, quantity, validity) with agreed payment terms and commitments.

2

Limina Facilitates & Matches

LIMINA securely processes the PO, matches it with supplier capabilities, or forwards it to relevant merchants. Suppliers provide their catalogue data.

3

Supplier Reviews & Commits

Suppliers review conditional POs, can accept terms, or await automatic matching if their standard pricing meets PO conditions.

4

Automated Execution & Settlement

Upon condition fulfillment, the order is automatically executed. LIMINA ensures secure payment settlement and order confirmation.
